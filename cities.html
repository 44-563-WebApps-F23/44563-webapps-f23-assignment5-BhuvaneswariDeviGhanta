Anantapur

Anantapur, officially Anantapuramu,[8] is a city in Anantapur district of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is the mandal headquarters of Anantapuru mandal and also the divisional headquarters of Anantapur revenue division.[9] The city is located on National Highway.
